Chapter One

Hobbies

Reading

I used to spend hours in my bedroom reading fiction novels while lying on the floor.

One of my favorite books was Oliver Twist by Charles Dickens. That story taught me gratitude and empathy.

Singing

I loved singing as a child and used my dad's old Nokia 3310 to record myself whenever I had a chance.

Even after hearing tough feedback, music stayed close to my heart and became part of my confidence journey.

Chapter Two

Favorite Toys

Playing Piano

My dad bought me a piano in grade 2, and I spent hours experimenting with sounds and simple melodies.

I once tried singing while playing, and even though it was difficult, it taught me patience and creativity.

Police Kit

My police kit was one of my favorite toys. It came with handcuffs, a baton, a radio, and a toy gun.

I still laugh when I remember cuffing my cousin and then panicking because I could not find the key.

Built with nostalgia and a smile.
